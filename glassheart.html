Triad of The Spirit Star

Synopsis

Glass Heart is the tale of Anya, a young princess who arrives at the Garden of Crowns. An academy open to the residents of Hedona with the express goal of grooming them to become diplomats, artists, scholars and, in Anya’s case, an alchemist.

Her idyllic life is interrupted one night as she is thrusted into a conflict between two princes from a neighbouring kingdom and a monstrous figure seeking an ancient power nestled within the heart of one of the princes.

Worse still, more witches are on their way as they're pulled from beyond the realm of the dead to the the bidding of a mysterious foe.

Now Anya and the princes must undo the curses plaguing their hallways as well as unravel the secrets of the past. If they fail, the entire fate of the world will fall to entropy.

About The Game

Triad of the Spirit Star centers around three main protagonist: Kasmiera Odele; Guardian of the Earth Star Venus, Shadae Lang; Guardian of the Heaven Star Elysia and Dino Joshi; Guardian of the Hell Star Pandemonia(More info about the team in the characters section).

Each of these girls has been chosen by one of the keepers for their specific personalities and are granted distinct powers based on their star.

Triad of the Spirit Star, follows the traditional monster of the week structure for each episode. A Spirit enters the human realm and possesses one of their classmates and they in turn must use their unique skills to find out what the Spirit wants (their Rule) and eventually send it back to the Spirit realm.

I wanted to have this series feel like a classic magic girl show that aired in the 90s with an emphasis on wacky hijinks, romance and a little darkness every now and then. Speaking of anime:

Gameplay

Since this is a visual novel, there will be the obvious dialogue scenes as well as choices to make, though I don't want the choices to be dialogue based. Instead, I want Anya to use a bunch of items stored within her inventory or snippets of information she gathered throughout the story to make certain events unfold.


Some work I was doing on the saving and loading system for the game. The backgrounds were temporary and have been replaced.

For example; If she overhears two people talking about another person, I want her to be able to store that information for use at a later stage. She can also collect objects that you might need at a later date, especially herbs and flowers for spells.

This brings us to the second type of gameplay I want to implement; exploration. I've already began work on this feature as you can see in the gif below, but basically Anya will be able to go from room to room by rotating the camera and clicking on doors. The wand will glow on objects of note and doorways as a handy guide so that you're easily alerted to what is clickable.

The third type of gameplay will be potion making. Since Anya is an aspiring alchemist, she's able to use guides and spell books to whip up all sort of potions that will allow her to access different rooms or defend against obstacles. Invisibility is one of those, but I'm hoping to have a whole list of spells planned out by the next dev log.



The final type of gameplay is still up in the air, but I might do an RPG battle system for cases where characters need to fend against witches and other foes, but we'll see.

There's also going to be dress-up game elements but those aren't as important. I just want to create a lot of pretty outfits for the characters. It's very self-indulgent.

Controls

To go to the next line of dialogue LEFT CLICK or PRESS SPACE. The wand disappears when the mouse isn't moved for a more immersive experience.

To open the menu RIGHT CLICK. There you can enable Auto Read as we as changing the scroll speed and other dialogue options. This is also where you'll also be able to SAVE and LOAD the game.

To view your history PRESS H and to close your history PRESS H AGAIN.

To navigate within exploration mode LEFT CLICK the left side of the screen to move left and LEFT CLICK the right side of the screen to move right. When the wand sparkles it means you can interact with the object. This might mean going to another room or inspecting an object closer.
